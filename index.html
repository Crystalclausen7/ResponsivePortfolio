About me

Hi I'm Crystal! I love learning new things and incorporating the things I learn in a creative way. I enjoy reading, writing, drawing, watching Doctor Who, Invader Zim and Scooby Doo, making videos and playing games. Coding has been a new learning experience and I can't wait to turn my creativity and coding into a new art for all to enjoy.

My love for Dark Art is a huge impact on all my artistic work that I do! I love the warped sense of humor especially in movies! One of my favorite movies is Arsenic and Old Lace. It has made a huge impression on my love for movies and want to make Film Noirs like that. I think that's why I love to incorporate everything I do into coding. That way I can make the websites for my future projects and share my love of all art with everyone.
